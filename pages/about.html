Our Story

Building Something Meaningful

The Vision

Lighthouse Design Build was born from a desire to create a better, more client-centered renovation experience while continuing a longstanding family legacy in construction. When Jason's father prepared to retire, Jason and his wife, Kristin, saw an opportunity to build something meaningful—something that reflected their values, their love of the water, and their commitment to homeowners.

The name Lighthouse is deeply personal. To our family, lighthouses represent home, safety, and direction. They guide you through fog, warn of danger, and lead you toward calmer waters. In the same way, our mission is to guide clients through the renovation process with confidence, transparency, and steady support from start to finish.

Jason's approach to design and construction is shaped by early jobsite experience, mentorship from his father, and formal education in architecture and finance. He spent five years running the family business before launching Lighthouse with Kristin, who provides high-level oversight of mission, culture, and long-term direction.

Jason continues to lean on his father for design insights and problem-solving, carrying forward the craftsmanship, integrity, and practical wisdom that defined his family's work for decades.
