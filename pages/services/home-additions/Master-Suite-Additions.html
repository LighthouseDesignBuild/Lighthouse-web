Master Suite Additions

A thoughtfully designed master suite addition transforms your home by creating a private retreat dedicated to comfort, relaxation, and refined living. At Lighthouse Design Build, we specialize in designing and constructing luxurious master suites that blend seamlessly with your existing home while elevating both functionality and value. Whether you are looking to expand your square footage or reimagine how you use your space, a master suite addition offers a tailored solution that enhances everyday living.

A master suite should feel like a destination within your home. It is where your day begins and ends, and it deserves the same level of attention and craftsmanship as any other significant investment. Our team approaches each project with careful planning, detailed design development, and expert construction practices to ensure your new space meets your expectations for comfort, beauty, and long-term durability.

Why Invest in a Master Suite Addition

A master suite addition is more than extra square footage. It is an opportunity to improve privacy, increase storage, and create a cohesive layout that supports your lifestyle. Many homeowners choose to add a master suite to accommodate changing needs, growing families, or a desire for more refined living spaces.

Key benefits of a master suite addition include:

  • Increased home value and market appeal
  • Enhanced privacy from other bedrooms and living areas
  • Improved functionality with modern layouts and storage solutions
  • Greater comfort through custom features and finishes
  • A personalized retreat designed around your preferences

At Lighthouse Design Build, we ensure that every master suite addition integrates architecturally with your home’s style while offering modern amenities that reflect today’s standards of luxury.

Spacious Bedroom Designs

The bedroom is the heart of any master suite addition. It should feel open, inviting, and proportioned for both comfort and elegance. Our design team carefully evaluates your home’s layout to create a spacious bedroom that feels connected yet private.

We consider factors such as natural light, ceiling height, furniture placement, and traffic flow to deliver a balanced and functional design. Features often included in our spacious bedroom designs include:

  • Large windows for natural light and ventilation
  • Tray or vaulted ceilings for added architectural interest
  • Integrated lighting plans for layered illumination
  • Built-in shelving or custom millwork
  • Direct access to outdoor spaces when possible

Each master suite addition is tailored to your lifestyle. Whether you prefer a minimalist aesthetic or a more traditional and detailed approach, we ensure that the bedroom serves as a calm and comfortable sanctuary.

Ensuite Bathroom with Luxury Finishes

A defining feature of any master suite addition is the ensuite bathroom. This space should combine practicality with spa-inspired luxury. Our team designs bathrooms that prioritize both daily convenience and indulgent comfort.

We collaborate with you to select materials and finishes that reflect your taste while maintaining durability and timeless appeal. Popular features in our ensuite bathroom designs include:

  • Walk-in showers with frameless glass enclosures
  • Freestanding soaking tubs
  • Double vanities with custom cabinetry
  • High-end tile and stone surfaces
  • Heated flooring systems
  • Thoughtfully designed lighting and mirrors

Functionality is just as important as aesthetics. We carefully plan plumbing layouts, ventilation, and storage solutions to ensure that your bathroom performs as beautifully as it looks. The result is a private space where you can unwind, refresh, and recharge.

Walk-In Closet Systems

A well-designed walk-in closet is an essential component of a master suite addition. Beyond simple storage, it should offer organization, accessibility, and a sense of order that simplifies your daily routine.

Our custom walk-in closet systems are tailored to your wardrobe and lifestyle. We evaluate your storage needs and incorporate features such as:

  • Adjustable shelving and hanging systems
  • Built-in drawers and compartments
  • Shoe and accessory displays
  • Integrated lighting
  • Island storage when space allows

By designing a closet that maximizes every square foot, we ensure that your master suite addition supports both functionality and style. The goal is to create a streamlined environment where everything has its place.

Private Sitting Areas

For homeowners seeking an elevated experience, a private sitting area adds an additional layer of comfort to a master suite addition. This flexible space can serve as a quiet reading nook, a small home office, or a place to relax at the end of the day.

We design sitting areas that complement the bedroom layout while maintaining a sense of intimacy. Depending on available space and your preferences, features may include:

  • Built-in window seats
  • Fireplace accents
  • Custom cabinetry
  • Soft lighting for a relaxed ambiance
  • Access to a balcony or patio
