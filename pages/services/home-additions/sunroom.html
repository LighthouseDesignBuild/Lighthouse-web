Expand Your Living Space with Purpose

A thoughtfully designed sunroom transforms unused exterior space into a functional extension of your home. Unlike basic enclosures, professionally built sunroom additions are integrated into your existing architecture to feel seamless and intentional. Our team carefully evaluates your home’s layout, structural requirements, and design aesthetic to ensure the final result looks and feels like it has always belonged there.

Sunrooms are ideal for:

  • Casual family living areas
  • Morning coffee and reading spaces
  • Entertaining guests in a light-filled setting
  • Indoor gardens and plant displays
  • Flexible bonus rooms for work or hobbies

By bringing in natural light through expansive windows, sunroom additions create an uplifting atmosphere that supports relaxation, productivity, and connection.

Designed for Year-Round Comfort

One of the most common concerns homeowners have about sunrooms is seasonal usability. At Lighthouse Design Build, we address this from the beginning of the design process. Our sunroom additions are constructed with high-performance materials and advanced climate solutions that allow you to enjoy the space throughout the year.

Key comfort features include:

Climate Control Systems

We design sunrooms with integrated heating and cooling options that maintain a consistent interior temperature. Depending on your home’s existing systems and layout, this may include extending HVAC, installing ductless mini split systems, or incorporating energy-efficient radiant heating. Proper ventilation and air circulation are also considered to ensure lasting comfort.

Insulated Glass Options

Modern insulated glass technology dramatically improves energy performance. We offer high-quality, double-pane and triple-pane insulated glass options that help regulate interior temperatures while reducing glare and UV exposure. This keeps your sunroom cooler in summer, warmer in winter, and more energy efficient year-round.

By combining insulation, glass performance, and climate control, our sunroom additions function as true living spaces rather than seasonal enclosures.

Floor-to-Ceiling Windows That Maximize Natural Light

The defining feature of most sunroom additions is expansive glazing. Floor-to-ceiling windows create unobstructed views of your yard, garden, or surrounding landscape. These large glass panels invite natural light deep into the space, reducing reliance on artificial lighting and creating a welcoming, open feel.

We carefully design window placement to optimize:

  • Daylight exposure
  • Privacy
  • Ventilation
  • Energy efficiency
  • Architectural balance

Framing materials and finishes are selected to complement your home’s style, whether traditional, transitional, or modern. The result is a bright, cohesive space that feels connected to the outdoors while remaining fully protected from the elements.

Seamless Indoor and Outdoor Transitions

A well-designed sunroom serves as a bridge between your interior living areas and the outdoor environment. We prioritize smooth indoor and outdoor transitions that enhance flow and functionality. This may include:

  • Sliding or folding glass doors
  • Wide cased openings
  • Coordinated flooring materials
  • Covered patios or decks directly adjacent to the sunroom

By thoughtfully connecting your sunroom to the rest of your home, we create a natural extension of your living space. This improves everyday usability and makes entertaining more enjoyable. Guests can move easily between the main living area, the sunroom, and outdoor spaces without disruption.
