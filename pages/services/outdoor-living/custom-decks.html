Designed Around Your Lifestyle

Every homeowner uses their outdoor space differently. Some prioritize large entertaining areas with integrated seating. Others want a private escape with layered landscaping and subtle lighting. Our design process begins with understanding how you live. We evaluate your lot, elevation, sun exposure, traffic flow, and architectural style to ensure your custom deck feels like a natural extension of your home.

During the planning phase, we collaborate closely with you to define priorities such as:

  • Dining and entertainment zones
  • Outdoor kitchens or grilling areas
  • Lounge spaces with fire features
  • Shade structures or pergolas
  • Privacy enhancements
  • Access points and transitions to the yard

This intentional planning ensures your deck is not just attractive but highly functional.

Wood Decking Options

Natural wood remains a timeless choice for homeowners who appreciate warmth, texture, and classic appeal. Our wood custom decks are built with quality materials and expert installation techniques to ensure longevity and structural integrity.

Popular wood options include pressure-treated lumber, cedar, and other premium hardwoods selected for durability and appearance. Benefits of wood decking include:

  • Natural beauty with rich grain patterns
  • Custom staining and finishing options
  • Traditional aesthetic that complements many home styles
  • Cost-effective material choices depending on species

We guide you through proper maintenance expectations and finishing options so your investment continues to look its best year after year.
